PETITIONS TO SIGN & INFO

Want to know how else you can support the AAPI (Asian American and Pacific Islander) community without worrying about what it will cost you? The "Petitions to Sign & Info" page aims to give you a list of petitions dedicated to supporting those part of the community who need the help. As of writing, none of the following petitions have reached their targeted number of signatures. Note that donations are optional when signing a petition but not encouraged since Change.org uses donations to fund their website in addition to advertising petitions. Sharing the petitions yourself will be more beneficial!

Trigger warning: This page and the petitions contain mentions of murder, police brutality, and other forms of violence.

NOTE: All of the petitions beside are on a petition website called Change.org. After signing, you will be asked if you wish to "chip in" so that the petition will reach more people. Although it is optional anyway, donating to Change.org is not encouraged.

The money will go toward advertising their website instead of directly helping the petition organizers. Donating to the organizations listed in the "Organizations to Support" page is more advisable.

Nevertheless, signing these petitions will still make a difference! Some of these petitions also have GoFundMe links on their pages where you can donate to the organizers themselves!

National Action against Police Brutality: Prosecute Police who Murder Unarmed Individuals

This campaign was started by Danette Chavis whose son, Gregory Chavis, died due to police brutality in 2004. This petition aims to demand action and to hold those who commit police brutality and murder accountable for their actions.

Sign this petition here.

Provide Safety Ambassadors & Prevent Hate Crimes on Elderly in San Francisco Chinatown

This petition urges local officials to create a Chinatown Community Benefits District (CBD) where Safety Ambassadors will be trained and employed as a response to the violence and discrimination the elderly Asian community is facing.

Sign this petition here.

End Hate Crimes against Asian Americans

A 90-year-old Asian grandmother was once attacked in broad daylight. This petition aims to have the New York Police Department (NYPD) investigate the attack and to request officials to pass legislation that will help against hate crimes.

Sign this petition here.

Demanding Justice for Christian Hall

Christian Hall was a 19-year-old Asian American who was a victim of police brutality while experiencing a mental health emergency. This petition urges officials to conduct an investigation and to prosecute the officers responsible.

Sign this petition here.

Stop Violence & Abuse against Chinese & Asian People

This petition is to the Southampton City Council, the United Kingdom (UK) Parliament, and the United Nations. It calls for local governments to take action and to conduct investigations for hate incidents against the Asian community.

Sign this petition here.

Pass the COVID-19 Hate Crimes Act

This petition aims to have the Congress pass the COVID-19 Hate Crimes Act, created in May of 2020. The Act, once passed, will help improve the reporting process of hate crimes and make this information available to the AAPI community.

Sign this petition here.

Ben Crump's Action Center

Ben Crump is a renowned American civil rights and personal injury attorney. Visiting the website for his Action Center will list a number of petitions you can sign with some information about the objectives of the petitions, including, but not limited to, seeking justice for police brutality victims.

Visit this site here.
