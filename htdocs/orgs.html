ORGANIZATIONS TO SUPPORT

Many organizations have more information about AAPI (Asian American and Pacific Islander) Hate, and a lot of them rely on donations in order to directly help victims, conduct researches and reports related to AAPI Hate, and do more things! The "Organizations to Support" page aims to provide a list of trusted organizations and networks that you can donate to which focus on helping Asians, Asian Americans, and Pacific Islanders! Simply visiting their websites will also be beneficial if you wish to learn more. Clicking on the organizations' names will lead you to their websites while hovering over their blocks will show more details about their organization.

ASIAN WOMEN ALLIANCE

The Asian Women Alliance (AWA) is a network of professionals and talent who want to use their platforms to help raise awareness and generate donations.

100% of the donations to the AWA will be used to fund nonprofit organizations that focus on helping the AAPI community, especially women, elders, and youth. On their website is also a donations list.

ASIAN AMERICANS ADVANCING JUSTICE

The Asian Americans Advancing Justice-Asian Law Caucus is the first Asian American legal and civil rights organization in the United States of America, and they fight for justice.

They focus on "serving low-income, immigrant, and underserved Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders," including those of Arab, Middle Eastern, and Muslim communities.

STOP AAPI HATE

The Stop AAPI Hate organization is a coalition formed by the AAPI Equity Alliance , Chinese for Affirmative Action , and the Asian American Studies Department of San Francisco State University .

It is a nonprofit organization that conducts researches and runs a center where people can report any hate and discrimination against those part of the AAPI community.

EMPOWERING PACIFIC ISLANDER COMMUNITIES

The Empowering Pacific Islander Communities (EPIC) national organization was established by a group of young, concerned Native Hawaiian and Pacific Islander (NHPI) leaders.

The EPIC organization is committed to social justice, with a mission to use research, leadership development, and culture-centered advocacy in order to serve and help the NHPI community.

FILIPINO COMMUNITY CENTER

The Filipino Community Center (FCC) is a grassroots organization that is dedicated to helping Filipino families and communities both in the United States of America and in the Philippines.

The FCC is has programs and services so that Filipinos can easily access inexpensive resources, like those for health, education, employment, immigration needs, service referrals, and more.

HEART OF DINNER

The Heart of Dinner organization is dedicated to serving the elderly Asian community in New York City by delivering care packages comprised of a hot lunch, fresh produce, and a wholesome letter.

The contents of the care packages are from gracious donations from the community and delicious food from local business owners. As of writing, 100,000 meals have been delivered.

NATIONAL AAPI MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION

The National Asian American Pacific Islander Mental Health Association (NAAPIMHA) is dedicated to raising awareness for and promoting the mental well-being of the AAPI community.

Visiting the NAAPIMHA website will also show you the training programs they have conducted, resources to educate if you want to learn more, and their most recent project "Asians*inFocus."

SOUTHEAST ASIAN COMMUNITY ALLIANCE

The Southeast Asian Community Alliance (SEACA) aims to use leadership development, advocacy, education, and community organizing in order to help the Southeast Asian community.

The SEACA conducts weekly interactive workshops for Southeast Asian youth through their "Youth Leadership Project." Their website also includes a blog where you can read about the community.

AAPIs FOR CIVIC EMPOWERMENT-EDUCATION FUND

The Asian American Pacific Islanders for Civic Empowerment-Education Fund (AAPI FORCE-EF) aims to raise awareness for policies and campaigns that support working-class AAPI people.

The AAPI FORCE-EF uses integrated voter engagement to educate AAPI voters through outreach strategies. Their website also includes resources and recent news updates.
